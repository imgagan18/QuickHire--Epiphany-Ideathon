About Us

QuickHire is a application that connects daily wage workers with businesses, streamlining the hiring process and providing financial stability for workers. Our goal is to provide workers with access to job opportunities, resources, and benefits, while also improving the efficiency of the hiring process for businesses.



At QuickHire, we believe in the power of technology to drive positive change. That's why we have designed our platform to offer the following benefits:

  • A real-time database of job opportunities across a range of industries and skill levels.
  • Direct payment from businesses to workers through our secure payment system.
  • An easy-to-use platform for workers to find and apply for jobs, and for businesses to post job listings and find and hire workers.

At QuickHire, we are committed to improving the lives of daily wage workers and helping businesses achieve their goals. Join us today and be a part of our mission to create a more efficient, effective, and equitable hiring process.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is QuickHire?

    QuickHire is a web application that connects daily wage workers directly with businesses, cutting out the middleman. Our goal is to provide employment opportunities and make the hiring process easier for both workers and businesses.

  • Who can use QuickHire?

    Both daily wage workers and businesses can use QuickHire. Our platform is designed for anyone looking for work or seeking to hire workers for their business.

  • Is QuickHire free to use?

    Yes, our Free Plan is completely free and provides access to all the basic features of QuickHire, including job listings and the ability to apply for jobs.

  • How much does QuickHire charge for its services?

    Our commission fees vary depending on the pricing plan you choose. Our Free Plan is completely free but you can hire a limited amount of people, while our Premium Plan will offer you consultancy service and ablity to hire more than 100+ people.

  • How does QuickHire differ from other job platforms?

    QuickHire cuts out the middleman and connects workers directly with businesses. We also offer a simple and straightforward pricing model, with the goal of making daily wage work accessible for everyone.
